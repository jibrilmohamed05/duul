Travel Guide for Somali Travelers

Everything you need to know before flying home

🛂

Arriving in Mogadishu

  • Somali passport holders: no visa required
  • Foreign nationals: visa on arrival available
  • Yellow fever certificate required from endemic countries
  • USD is widely accepted — exchange at the airport
🧳

Baggage & Customs

  • Most airlines: 23kg checked + 7kg carry-on
  • Declare cash over $10,000 USD
  • Electronics and personal items generally allowed
  • Wrap gifts loosely — customs may inspect
📱

Connectivity & Money

  • Hormuud SIM cards available at the airport
  • 4G coverage strong in Mogadishu
  • EVC Plus mobile money is widely used
  • ATMs are limited — carry USD cash
🔒

Safety & Preparation

  • Register with your country's embassy on arrival
  • Share your itinerary with family before traveling
  • Use vetted transport only
  • Check your government's travel advisory first
Pro Tips

10 Things to Know Before You Fly

1

Arrive at the airport early

Allow 3 hours before departure — security and check-in can take longer.

2

Download your boarding pass

Print or save a PDF backup — airport WiFi can be unreliable.

3

Carry small USD bills

$1–$20 bills are useful for tips and informal transport.

4

Share your flight number

Give family your exact flight number so they can track your arrival.

5

Check weight limits carefully

Overweight fees at the airport are costly — check before you pack.
